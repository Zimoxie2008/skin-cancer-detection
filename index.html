Skin Cancer Detection Project

Overview

Aim

Our goal is to explore how augmenting image data can help a deep learning model more accurately detect skin cancer in real-world scenarios, making it more accessible and affordable for people worldwide.

Method

We are training a deep learning model using augmented skin lesion images to improve its ability to generalize across various environmental conditions. Our research focuses on exploring different image augmentations and optimization techniques to improve model performance.

Variables

Independent Variable: Image augmentations (e.g., rotation, zooming, flipping).
Dependent Variable: Model accuracy, loss, and AUC score.

Additional Information

Our project aims to reduce unnecessary dermatological visits and ensure early detection of skin cancer in underserved areas.
